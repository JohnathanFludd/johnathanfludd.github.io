As a former musician, I'm interested in:

  • Discovering new ways to find new music
  • Cultivating a DIY mindset in young musicians

About Johnathan

My passion is all things audio and sound design. Working with audio is something I'm already familiar with as I do a lot of music production on the side. Finding a way to implement the two into my web design career is something that I look forward to achieving.

I am a creative person who loves to work on various projects. I understand how web design can open up a lot of doors to work on things I'm interested in as well as new interests that I have yet to discover. I love the collaborative aspect of coding and enjoy working in groups to finish projects.
